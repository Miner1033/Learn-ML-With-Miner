Artificial Neural Networks (ANN)

What is an ANN?

English: An Artificial Neural Network (ANN) is a computing system inspired by the human brain's structure. It consists of layers of connected nodes ("neurons") that process data by passing signals through weights and activation functions. ANNs are used to recognize patterns, classify data, and predict outcomes.
